Royal O’ Brien

GM of Digital Media and Games, Linux Foundation and Executive Director, Open 3D Foundation

Royal is the General Manager of Digital Media and Games at the Linux Foundation as well as the Executive Director of the Open 3D Foundation, with over 30 years of experience in the corporate enterprise and video game industries, having founded multiple companies from software development to service solutions.

He has multiple patents in both video, telephony & digital distribution technologies. He has invented and holds multiple issued patents in the video, VOIP, game, and digital distribution technology industries and has successfully commercialized multiple products in the marketplace from theory to final release. He has also proudly served our nation as a US Marine.

Nicole Huesman

Director of Marketing and Community Outreach

Nicole is the Director of Marketing and Community Outreach for the Open 3D Foundation, home to a vibrant, diverse community focused on advancing open 3D technologies and development. As a storyteller in tech for nearly three decades, Nicole has helped shine a light on the broader impact of advances across both software and hardware. She dove into the world of open source at Intel over a dozen years ago, quickly becoming a strong advocate.

She is passionate about cultivating inclusive communities that welcome diverse perspectives, contributing to the CHAOSS Project and moderating, speaking and blogging in the areas of diversity and mentorship. She holds an MBA degree from Marylhurst University, with an emphasis in Marketing, and a BA from the University of Oregon’s School of Journalism.
