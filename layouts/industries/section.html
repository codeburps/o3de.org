INDUSTRIES

Full-fidelity simulations and visualizations, enabled through real-time 3D engines, bridge the physical and digital worlds, delivering immersive experiences to entertain and offering keen insights into the world around us.

While 3D technologies emerged in game development decades ago, their broad adoption has advanced diverse industries, unleashing exciting, new possibilities from animated movies to scientific exploration.

CORE INDUSTRIES

Gaming

Create stunning worlds that support multi-faceted storytelling and networked, multiplayer game play, across diverse screens and devices.

Robotics

Deploy O3DE in robotics applications through its integration with the Robotic Operating System (ROS), without the need for wrappers or bridges.

Education

Apply different learning modalities to meet the needs of the modern classroom, with 3D technology that enables deeper understanding and faster learning.

Metaverse

Deliver immersive experiences and 3D digital assets for the emerging world of the open metaverse.
