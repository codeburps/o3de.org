SPECIAL INTEREST GROUPS
(SIGS)

As O3DE is a very large project. Special Interest Groups (SIGs) help us divide up the planning and communication for the different areas and responsibilities around developing and maintaining the engine. Explore and join the ones that map best to your interests and experience.

SIMULATION
